Pallavi:

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
1. vadana dyuti jita sOma vasudhA mAnasa kAma
mada mAnava gaNa bhIma mAM pAhi shrI rAma (shO)


2. janaka sutA hRd-ramaNa jamadagnija mada haraNa
praNatAghAnala varuNa pAhi mAM muni sharaNa (shO)



3. vigaLita mOha pAsha vidhu kOTi saMkAsha
bhagavan sakalAdhIsha pAhi pApa vinAsha (shO)



4. vara tyAgarAja nuta vArija saMbhava tAta
parama kalyANa yuta pAhi mAM shubha carita (shO)
