nI cittamu nishcalamu

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: dhanyasi
Tala: chApu
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
nI cittamu nishcalamu
nirmalamani ninnE namminAnu

Anupallavi:
nA cittamu vancana cancalamani
nannu viDanADakumi shrI rAma (nI)

Charanam:
guruvu cilla ginja guruvE bhramaramu
guruDE bhAskaruDu guruDE bhadruDu
guruDE uttama gati guruvu nIvanukoNTi
dharanu dAsuni brOva tyAgarAja nuta (nI)

Kannada Lyrics
