lIlagAnu jUcu

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: divyamani - dundubhi
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
lIlagAnu jUcu guNa shIlula nA
pala kalga jEsi pAlimpumayya

Anupallavi:
pAlumAlina pAmarulanolla
paramAtmayI lOkamulanu (lIla)

Charanam:
nara kinnara kimpuruShAsura
nirjara rAja shivAdi ramA patula
taru bhU-dharAnEkANDamula shrI
tyAgarAja nuta shrI rAma nI (lIla)

Kannada Lyrics

ರಚನೆ: ತ್ಯಾಗರಾಜ
