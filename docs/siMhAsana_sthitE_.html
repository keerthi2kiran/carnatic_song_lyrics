siMhAsana sthitE

English Lyrics

Composer: muthuswami dikshitar
Raga: ragamalika - mangala ragamalika - veda ragamalika
Tala: rupakam
Language: sanskrit

Pallavi:
siMhAsana sthitE sundara guru guha nuta lalitE
cidAnanda nAthO(a)haM vandE(a)haM vandE(a)ham

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
brahmAdi sakala dEvatArAdhita pada yugaLE
parashu rAma parama bhAgavata pramukhOpAsita bagaLEkamalEsha nuta maMgaLE kavi jana nata maMgaLE
mantriNI daNDinI samUhAdi maMgaLEhaya-grIva kumbhajAdi pUjita nitya maMgaLE
niratishaya shubha maMgaLE nava nava vidha maMgaLE
