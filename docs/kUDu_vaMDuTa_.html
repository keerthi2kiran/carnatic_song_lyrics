kUDu vaMDuTa

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: sri
Tala: ?
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
kUDu vaMDuTa gaMjikorakA tanaku |
vEDukalu gala sukhamu vedukuTaku gAka ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
kuppa nurucuTa kasavukorakA tanuvu |
goppayavuTidi madamu koraka |
oppayina vEDukala norasi manasu |
neppunaku rAdiviya nEravale gAka ||

kolucu daMcuTa poTTakorakA tA- |
gulajuDai mUDhuMDau korakA |
talapOsi yinniTa dagili mIdu |
telisi sukhaduHkhamula deliyavalegAka ||

koMDa davvuTa yelukakorakA tA- |

koMDala kOnETipati golici tanaku |
niMDi nApadalel a nIgavale gAka ||
