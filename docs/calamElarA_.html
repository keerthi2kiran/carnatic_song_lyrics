calamElarA

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: margahindolam
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
calamElarA sAkEta rAma

Anupallavi:
valaci bhakti mArgamutOnu ninnu
varNincucunna nAtO (calamu)

Charanam:
endu pOdu nEnEmi sEyudunu
eccOTa nE mora peTTudunu
dandanalatO proddu pOvalenA
tALa jAlarA tyAgarAja nuta (calamu)
