sItApatE nA manasuna

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: khamas
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
sItApatE nA manasuna
siddhAntamani yunnAnurA

Anupallavi:
vAtAtmajAdula centanE
varNincina nI palukulella (sItA)

Charanam:
prEma jUci nApai pedda manasu jEsi
nI mahimalella niNDAra jUpi
I mahini bhayamETikanna mATa
rAmacandra tyAgarAja vinuta (sItApatE)
