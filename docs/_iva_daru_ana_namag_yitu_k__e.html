śiva daruśana namagāyitu kēḷe

English Lyrics

Composer: Purandara Dasa
Raga:
Tala:
Language: kannada

Pallavi:

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
śiva daruśana namagāyitu kēḷe
śivarātriya jāgaraṇe ||

pātāḷa gaṅgeya snānava māḍalu
pātakavella parihāravu
jyōtirliṅgana dhyānava māḍalu
dyūtagaḷilla anudinavu ||

bēḍiva varagaḷa koḍuvanu tāyi
brahmana rāṇiya nōḍuvanu
āḍuta pāḍuta ēruta basavana
ānandadali nalidāḍuvanu ||

śikharava kaṇḍenu purandaraviṭhalana
harinārāyaṇa dhyānadale ||
