pAhi rAmacandra pAlita

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: sankarabharanam
Tala: Adi
Language: sanskrit

Pallavi:
pAhi rAmacandra pAlita surEndra
parama pAvana sadguNa sAndra

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
nIrada nIla munIndra hRdaya
nArada sEvita sArasa nayana (pA)shrIkara rUpa sudhAkara vadana
shOka nivAraNa sundara radana (pA)nirmala rUpa nindita madana
sharmada sakalEshArNava sadana (pA)rAjarAja nuta rAghava tyAga-
rAja hRdAlaya rakShita nAga
