mAra vairi ramaNI

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: nasika bhushani
Tala: rupakam
Language: sanskrit

Pallavi:
mAra vairi ramaNI manju bhAShiNI

Anupallavi:
krUra dAnavEbha vAraNAri gaurI (mAra)

Charanam:
1 kAma bandha vAraNa niSh-kAma citta varadE
dharma saMvardhani sadA vadana hAsE
tyAgarAja shubha phaladE (mAra)
