idi samayamurA

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: chaya nata
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
idi samayamurA ina kula tilaka

Anupallavi:
1 vidaLita kali durmada gaja gamana
modaTa palkinadi nijamuga jEya (idi)

Charanam:
kali puruShuDu nATakamunu kaTTa
talacinADu shrI tyAgarAja nuta
khala matamulanE yAgamulaku
mEkaluga narula jEsi nI daya jUDa (idi)
