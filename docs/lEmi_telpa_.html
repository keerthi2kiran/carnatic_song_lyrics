lEmi telpa

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: navaneetam
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
lEmi telpa peddalevaru lErO

Anupallavi:
1 A mahimalella mAni I mahilO puTTirO (lEmi)

Charanam:
2 nI rUpamunu pUni manasu nirmalulaiyuNDa lEdA
dhArA dharAbha sharIra tyAgarAja vinuta (lEmi)

Kannada Lyrics

ತಾಳ: ಆದಿ

ಚರಣ:
