bRndAvana lOla

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: thodi
Tala: rupakam
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
bRndAvana lOla gOvindAravinda nayana

Anupallavi:
sundarAMga dhRta rathAMga
sujanAri timira pataMga (bRndAvana)

Charanam:
mAmava shrI ramaNI maNi madanAshrita mitra
rAma dAsa dAsa tyAgarAja nuta caritra (bRndAvana)

Kannada Lyrics
