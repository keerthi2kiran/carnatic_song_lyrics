adi kAdu bhajana

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: yadukula kambhoji
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
adi kAdu bhajana manasA

Anupallavi:
edalOnencuTokaTi
payyeda kalginaconokaTi (adi)

Charanam:
goppa tanamukai yAsa
kutsita viShaya pipAsa
meppulakai bahu vEsamiDi
ubbatilledaru tyAgarAja vinuta (adi)
