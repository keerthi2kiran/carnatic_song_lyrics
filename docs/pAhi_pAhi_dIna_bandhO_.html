pAhi pAhi dIna bandhO

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: saurashtram
Tala: Jhampa
Language: sanskrit

Pallavi:
pAhi pAhi dIna bandhO mAm
pAhi parAnanda sindhO

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
1. tArakAsura vairi nuta rAma
tAraka shubha kara carita (pAhi)


2. brahma rudrAdi sabhEshArIbha
simhAnana jita bhEsha (pAhi)


3. shakrAri janaka samharaNa shaMkha
cakrAdi dharAshrita caraNa (pAhi)


4. rAghavAnvaya supradIpa su-
karAgha vana kuThAra rUpa (pAhi)

5. shara shara jagad-AdhAra akhil-
Ashara sharadAshuga dhIra (pAhi)


6. kuvalaya daLa sama nEtra pAlita
kuvalaya daLitAmitra (pAhi)

7. kamalA hita guNa bharita rAma
kamalAhita dhara vinuta (pAhi)

8.tyAgarAja nuta caraNa
nityAga rAja dhara suguNa (pAhi)
