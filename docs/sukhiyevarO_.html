sukhiyevarO

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: kanada
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
sukhiyevarO rAma nAma sukhiyevarO

Anupallavi:
sukhiyevarO sumukhiyevarO
akhila sAramagu tAraka nAma (sukhi)

Charanam:
satyamu tappaga sakala lOkulaku
bhRtyuDai daiva bhEdamu lEka
nityamaina su-svarapu gAnamutO
nirantaramu tyAgarAja nuta nAma (sukhi)
