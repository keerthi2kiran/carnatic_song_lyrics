AkaTi vELala

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: revati
Tala: ?
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
AkaTi vELala alupaina vELalanu |
tEkuva harinAmamE dikku mari lEdu ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
koramAliyunna vELa kulamu ceDina vELa |
cerapaDi vorulacE jikkina vELa |
vorapaina harinAma mokkaTE gatigAka |
maraci tappinanaina marilEdu teragu |Apada vaccina vELa yUraDibaDina vELa |
pApapu vELala bhayapaDina vELa |
vOpinanta harinAma mokkaTE gatigAka |
mApudAka boralina marilEdu teragu ||

maMkubuddhi poralina marilEdu teragu ||
