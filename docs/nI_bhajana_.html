nI bhajana

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: nayaki
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
nI bhajana gAna rasikula
nEnendu gAnarA rAma

Anupallavi:
shrI bhava sarOjaasanaadi
shacI manO-ramaNa vandya ilalO (nI)

Charanam:
saguNa nirguNapu nija dabbaralanu
ShaN-matamula marmamaShTa siddhula
vagalu jUpa santasilla kaNTini
varAnana tyAgarAja vinuta (nI)
