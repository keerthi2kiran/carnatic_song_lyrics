Pallavi:
paramAtmuDaina hari paTTapurANivi nIvu |
dharamamu vicAriMca tagunIku amma ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
kamalajuganna tallivi kAmuniganna talli |
amarulaganna talli Adima lakShmi |
vimalapu nIpatiki vinnapamu jEsi mammu |
nemaki Eliti daya nIkE tagunamma ||

kAmadhEnu tObuTTuga kalpakamu tObuTTuga |
dOmaTi callina caMdru tObuTTuga |
nImagani paMpunanu nijasiruliccitivi |
nEmapu vitaraNamu nIkE tagunamma ||

pAlajaladhi kanyavu padmAsinivi nIvu |
pAlapaMDE shrIvEMkaTapati dEvivi |
