rAmA nIyeDa prEma

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: dileepaka
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
1 rAmA nIyeDa prEma rahitulaku
nAma ruci telusunA O sItA

Anupallavi:
kAmini vESha dhAriki sAdhvI naDatal-
Emaina telusunA A rIti sItA (rAmA)

Charanam:
tana saukhyamu tAnerugakanorulaku
tagu bOdhana sukhamA
ghanamagu puli gO rUpamaitE tyAga-
rAja nuta shishuvuku pAlu kalgunA (rAmA)

Kannada Lyrics

ಅನುಪಲ್ಲವಿ:
