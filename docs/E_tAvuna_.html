E tAvuna

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: yadukula kambhoji
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
E tAvuna nErcitivO rAma endukinta gAsi

Anupallavi:
sItA lakShmaNa bharata ripughna
vAtAtmajulatOnADu nATakam(E tAvuna)

Charanam:
Alu vajrAla sommulaDigirO
anujulu tali-daNDrulannamaDigirO
shIlulaina vara bhaktulu pilicirO cira
kAlamu tyAgarAja nuta nIv(E tAvuna)

Kannada Lyrics

ರಚನೆ: ತ್ಯಾಗರಾಜ

ವಾತಾತ್ಮಜುಲತೋನಾಡು ನಾಟಕಮ್(ಏ ತಾವುನ)
