rAma rAma kali

English Lyrics

Composer: muthuswami dikshitar
Raga: ramakali
Tala: rupakam
Language: sanskrit

Pallavi:
rAma rAma kali kaluSha virAma
dharA bhRllalAma

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
shrI mahijA kAma suguNa dhAma
parandhAma shyAma
mAmava shiva guru guha sutrAma -
vibudha vinuta nAma
