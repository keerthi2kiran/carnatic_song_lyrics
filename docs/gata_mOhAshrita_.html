gata mOhAshrita

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: sankarabharanam
Tala: rupakam
Language: sanskrit

Pallavi:
gata mOhAshrita pAlAdbhuta sItA-ramaNa

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
bhava sArasabhava mAnasa bhavanAmara vinuta (gata)bhava-tAraka sava-pAlana bhavadAshara-haraNa (gata)
vinatAja-gamana rAghava muni-pUjita-caraNa (gata)shatakOTi carita mAnava mata bhEdaka damana (gata)
kara-shObhita-shara pApa-timira-bhAskara suguNa (gata)
sharajAnana karuNAkara vara vAraNa-sharaNa (gata)
nata-mAnasa hitakara pAlita tyAgarAja (gata)
