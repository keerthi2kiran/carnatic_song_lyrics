Language: telugu

Pallavi:

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
1. nI pAdamula bhakti niNDAraganosagi
kApADu nA pApamE pATi rAma (shrI)


2. paliki konnALLipuDu palukakuNTE viDutunA
kula dharmamulanella talaci nanu brOvavayya (shrI)

3. palumAru ninnu pADi pADi vEDukonnAnu
kalakalamani nAtO palukavEmayya ipuDu (shrI)


4. lOkula nera nammukonaka nI pAdamu-
lindAka nA madinunca krI kaNTa jUcedavEmi (shrI)

5. rAja shEkhara dEva rAja rAjIva bhava
pUjArha shrI tyAgarAjuni moralAlakincu (shrI)
