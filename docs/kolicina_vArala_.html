kolicina vArala

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: kedara
Tala: ?
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
kolicina vArala koMgupaiDitaDu |
balimi tAraka brahmamItaDu ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
inavaMShAMbudhi negasina tEjamu |
Ganayaj~jaMbula gala phalamu |
manujarUpamuna maniyeDi brahmamu |
ninuvula raGukula nidhAnamItaDu ||

paramAnnamulOpali sArapujavi |
paraginadivijula bhayaharamu |
mariginasItA maMgaLasUtramu |
dharalO rAmAvatAraMbitaDu ||

sakala vanacarula jayakaramu |
vikasitamagu shrIvEMkaTa nilayamu |
prakaTita daSharatha bhAgyaMbitaDu
