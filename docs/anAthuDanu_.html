anAthuDanu

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: jingala
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
anAthuDanu kAnu rAma nE-(nanAthuDanu)

Anupallavi:
anAthuDavu nIvani nigamajJula
sanAtanula mATa vinnAnu nE-(nanAthu)

Charanam:
nirAdaravu jUciyI kali
narAdhamulanedaru
purANa puruSha pura ripu nuta
nAga rAT-shayana tyAgarAja nuta nEn(anAthu)
