tulasI bilva

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: kedara gaula
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
tulasI bilva mallikAdi
jalaja sumamula pUjala kaikonavE

Anupallavi:
jalajAsana sanakAdi karArcita
jaladAbha su-nAbha vibhAkara hRj-
jalEsha hariNAMka sugandha (tulasI)

Charanam:
uramuna mukhamuna shiramuna bhujamuna
karamuna nEtramuna caraNa yugambuna
karuNatO nenarutO paramAnandamutO
niratamunu shrI tyAgarAju
nir-upAdhikuDai arcincu (tulasI)
