enna manada ḍoṅka tiddisō

English Lyrics

Composer: Purandara Dasa
Raga:
Tala:
Language: kannada

Pallavi:
enna manada ḍoṅka tiddisō śrīnivāsa
enna manada ḍoṅka tiddisō

Anupallavi:

enna manada ḍoṅka tiddisi
ninna sēvakanāda mēle
innu saṁśayavēke kṛṣṇa
ninna caraṇadalli sērisō

Charanam:


udayavādare ūṭada cinte
adara mēle bhōgada cinte
hadinālku lōkavāḷabēkemba cinte
idu puṇya idu pāpavendu
hṛdayadali bhayavillade
madanōhitanāde ninna
padava nambade dīnadayāḷō ||

neremaneya bhāgya nōḍi
sahisalārade asūyanāgi
harismaraṇege vimukhanāde
narara stutiya māḍide
parara satige para annakke
tirugi tirugi capalanāde
guruhiriyara dūṣiṇi
maruḷāde dīnaśaraṇya ||

agaṇita sukha bandare nā
agaṇita duḥkhakhariyembuve
jagadoḷu lābhavu bandare dhanika nānembe
mige hānige hari dūṣisi
negedu pataṅga kiccili bīḷva
bageyādenu purandara viṭhala
khagarāja suvāhana ||
