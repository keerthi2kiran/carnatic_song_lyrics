shyAma sundarAMga

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: dhanyasi
Tala: rupakam
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
shyAma sundarAMga sakala shaktiyu nIvErA

Anupallavi:
tAmasa rahita guNa sAndra
dharanu velayu rAmacandra (shyAma)

Charanam:
duShTa danuja mada vidAra
shiShTa jana hRdaya vihAra
iShTa daivamu nIvErA
ilanu tyAgarAju vErA (shyAma)
