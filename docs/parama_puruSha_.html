English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: ?
Tala: ?
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
parama puruSha hari parAtpara |
pararipu bhaMjana paripUrNa namO ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
kamalA pati kamala nAbha kamalAsana vaMdya |
kamala hitAnaMta kOTi Gana samudaya tEjA |
kamalAmala patranEtra kamalavairi varNagAtra |
kamalaShaTka yOgIshvara hrudayatEhaM namO namO ||

jalanidhi madhana jalanidhi baMdhana jaladhi madhya shayanA |
jaladhi yaMtara vihAra maccakaccapa yavatArA |
jalanidhi jAmAta jalanidhi shOShaNa jalanidhi saptakagamana |
jalanidhi kAruNya namO tEhaM jalanidhi gaMbhIra namO namO ||

nagadhara nagaripu naMdita nagacara yUthapanAthA |
naga pArijAta hara sArasa pannaga patirAja shayanA |
nagakula vijaya shrI vEMkaTa naga nAyaka bhakta vidhEyA |
nagadhIrAhaMtE sarvEshvara nArAyaNa namO namO ||
