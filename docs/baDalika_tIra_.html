baDalika tIra

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: reeti gaula
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
baDalika tIra pavvaLincavE

Anupallavi:
saDalani duritamulanu tega kOsi
sArvabhauma sAkEta rAma (baDalika)

Charanam:
paMkajAsanuni paritApamu kani
paMkajApta kula pativai velasi
paMkajAkShitO vanamunakEgi
jiMkanu vadhiyinci
maMku rAvaNuni madamunanaNaci
nishshaMkuDagu vibhIShaNuniki baMgAru
