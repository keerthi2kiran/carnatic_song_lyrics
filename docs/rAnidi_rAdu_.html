rAnidi rAdu

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: manirangu
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
rAnidi rAdu surAsurulakaina

Anupallavi:
pOnidi pOdu bhU-surulakaina (rAnidi)

Charanam:
dEvEndruniki su-dEhamu pUrva
dEvulakamRtamabhAvamE gAni
A vana cara bAdhalA munulakE gAni
pAvana tyAgarAja bhAgyamA shrI rAma (rAnidi)
