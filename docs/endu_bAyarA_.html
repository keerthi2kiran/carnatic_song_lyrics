endu bAyarA

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: dhanyasi
Tala: rupakam
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
endu bAyarA daya ina kula tilaka daya

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
1. indu vadana kunda radana
mandara dhara nI daya (endu)

2. shAnta bhUSha bhakta pOSha
sharaNu shESha shAyi (endu)

3. shrita sharaNya sura varENya
kRta su-puNya dhanya daya (endu)


4. nAga rAja dhara nutAja
tyAgarAja varada daya (endu)
