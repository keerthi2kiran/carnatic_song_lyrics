sItA vara

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: devagandhari
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
sItA vara saMgIta jnanamu dhAta vrAyavalerA

Anupallavi:
gItAdyakhilOpaniShat-sAra bhUta
jIvan-muktuDaguTaku (sItA)

Charanam:
AkAsha sharIramu brahmamanE
AtmArAmuni tA sari jUcucu
lOkAdulu cinmayamanu susvara
lOluDau tyAgarAja sannuta (sItA)
