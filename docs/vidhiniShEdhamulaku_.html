English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: devagandhari
Tala: ?
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
vidhiniShEdhamulaku veruvagabanilEdu |
madhusUdana nimannana dAsuDaitE ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
viDuvarAni dharmavidhula puruShulanu |
viDici gOpikalu viccanaviDi |
baDininnu daguluTE paramadharmamAya |
yeDayunitaradharmA likanETikayyA ||

mAnarAnikarmamArgamulaTu mAni |
pUninayatulE pUjyulaTa |
nInArAyaNaniyatE dharmamAya |
yInija mokaTiyu nerugagavalayu ||

yinniTa shrIvEMkaTEsha nIdAsuDai- |
vunna vicArAla noduganElA |
ninnugUrcinaTTi nijabhakti galadani |
tinnanai telipETitelivE kaladi ||
