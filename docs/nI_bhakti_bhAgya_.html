nI bhakti bhAgya

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: jaya manohari
Tala: rupakam
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
nI bhakti bhAgya sudhA
nidhinIdEdE janmamu

Anupallavi:
bhU-bhAramu kAni sura
bhU surulai janincina (nI)

Charanam:
vEdOktambau karmamu
veta galgu gatAgatamau
nAdAtmaka tyAgarAja
nAthApramEya sadA (nI)
