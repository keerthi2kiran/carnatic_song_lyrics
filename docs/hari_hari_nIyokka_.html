hari hari nIyokka

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: punnagavarali
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
hari hari nIyokka divya pAdAravindamiyyavE

Anupallavi:
dharanu kalugu bhOga bhAgyamellanu
tathyamu kAdu sumI shrI kRShNa

Charanam:
1.
sanaka sanandana shrI nArada
shukArjuna ghanulella nutincu

vinu vEda purANAgama shAstra
vidyalandu carincu
