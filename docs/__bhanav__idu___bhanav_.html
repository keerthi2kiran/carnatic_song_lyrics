Anupallavi:

Charanam:

vaibhavavē namma vāmanamūrtige ||

pālugaḍalu maneyāgiralu
āladeleya mēle malaguvare
mūrlōkava ninnudaradoḷimbiṭṭu muddu-
bālakanāgi ettisikombare ||

siri ninna kaivaśavāgiralu
tirivare baliya dānava bēḍi
sarasijabhava ninna pūje māḍutalire
narana baṇḍiya bōvanāguvare ||

kammagōlana pitanāgiralu
summane kubujege sōluvare
bomma mūruti ninageṇeyuṇṭe trijagadi
hammina dēva purandaraviṭhala ||
