tattvamariya

English Lyrics

Composer: pApanAsam sivan
Raga: reeti gaula
Tala: Adi
Language: tamizh

Pallavi:
tattvamariya taramA (nin)
mUlAdhAra-gaNapatE surapatE unadu

Anupallavi:
satvaguNamuM jIvadayaiyum jJAnamuM
sarrumillAda kirATanukku (unadu)

Charanam:
madhuraparipUrNa-mOdaka-karanE
mahAvighnavana-kUTara-varanE
nidhi-onbadum aNbarkaruLparaNE
nikhilacarAcara-bIjApUranE

matishEkharan maganE sumukhanE
madavAranamukhanE
shrutimuDi uNarvadu citparanE
guhasOdaranE rAmadAsan unadu
