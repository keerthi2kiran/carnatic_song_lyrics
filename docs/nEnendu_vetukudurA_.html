nEnendu vetukudurA

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: karnataka behag
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
nEnendu vetukudurA hari

Anupallavi:
A nAlgu mOmula vAni mora-
nAlaginci rAni ninnu (nEnendu)

Charanam:
kaluShAtmuDai duShkarma yutuDai
palumAru durbhAShiyai
ilalO bhaktAgrEsarula
vEShiyai tyAgarAja pUjita (nEnendu)
