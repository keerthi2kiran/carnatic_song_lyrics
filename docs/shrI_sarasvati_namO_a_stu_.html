shrI sarasvati namO(a)stu

English Lyrics

Composer: muthuswami dikshitar
Raga: arabhi
Tala: rupakam
Language: sanskrit

Pallavi:
shrI sarasvati namO(a)stu tE
varadE para dEvatE
(madhyama kAla sAhityam)
shrI pati gaurI pati guru guha vinutE
vidhi yuvatE

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
vAsanA traya vivarjita -
vara muni bhAvita mUrtE
vAsavAdyakhila nirjara -
vara vitaraNa bahu kIrtE dara -
(madhyama kAla sAhityam)
hAsa yuta mukhAmburuhE
adbhuta caraNAmburuhE
saMsAra bhItyApahE
sakala mantrAkShara guhE

ತಾಳ: ರೂಪಕ
ಭಾಷೆ: ಸಂಸ್ಕೃತ

ವರದೇ ಪರ ದೇವತೇ
(ಮಧ್ಯಮ ಕಾಲ ಸಾಹಿತ್ಯಮ್)
