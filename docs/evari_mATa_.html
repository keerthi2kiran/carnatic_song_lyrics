evari mATa

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: kambhoji
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
evari mATa vinnAvO rAvO indu lEvO bhaLi bhaLi

Anupallavi:
avanilOnArShEya pauruShEyam-
andi cOdyameruga lEnayya (evari)

Charanam:
bhakta parAdhInuDanucu
parama bhAgavatula
vyakta rUpuDai palikina muccaTa
yuktamanucunuNTi
shakti gala mahA-dEvuDu nIvani
santOShamunanuNTi
satta cittuDagu tyAgarAja nuta
satya sandhuDanukoNTinilalO (evari)
