rakSha peTTarE

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: bhairavi
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
1 rakSha peTTarE doraku

Anupallavi:
vakSha sthalamuna velayu
lakShmI ramaNuniki sAya (rakSha)

Charanam:
sItA karamunu paTTi celagina doraku
vAtAtmajuniki ceyi vashamaina doraku
puruhUtAdula rakShimpa bAhujuDaina doraku
saMgIta priya tyAgarAja gEyuDaina dorakaishvarya (rakSha)

Kannada Lyrics
