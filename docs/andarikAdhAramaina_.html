andarikAdhAramaina

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: samantha - bilahari
Tala: ?
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
andarikAdhAramaina Adi puruShuDItaDu |
vindai munnAragince vidurunikaDa nItaDu ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
sanakAdulu koniyADeDi sarvAtmakuDItaDu |
vanaja bhavAdulakunu daivambai nataDItaDu |
inamaNDalamuna jelagETihitavai bhavuDItaDu |
munupuTTina dEvatalaku mUlabhUti yItaDu ||

sirulosagi yashOdayinTa shishuvainata DItaDu |
dharanAvula mandalalO taga jarince nItaDu |
sarasatalanu gol etalaku janavulosage nItaDu |
Arasi kucEluni yaDukulu AragincenItaDu ||

paMkajabhavunakunu brahma pada mosagenu yItaDu |
saMkIrtana lAdyulacE jaTTi goniyenItaDu |

vEMkaTagiri mIda prabhala velasina ghanuDItaDu ||
