sarasIruha nayanE

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: amrta varshini
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
sarasIruha nayanE sarasijAsanE
sharadindu nibha vadanE amba

Anupallavi:
parama kRpA nidhi nIvEyani ninu
niratamu nammiti brOvavE talli (sara)

Charanam:
1 svara rAga laya shruti marmambulu
nAradAdi munulakupadEshincE
vara dAyaki amRta varShiNi
maravaku lalitE tyAgarAja nutE (sara)

Kannada Lyrics

ತಾಳ: ಆದಿ

ಅನುಪಲ್ಲವಿ:
