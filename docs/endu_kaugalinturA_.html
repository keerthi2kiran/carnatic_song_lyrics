endu kaugalinturA

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: suddha desi
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
endu kaugalinturA ninn-
entani varNinturA ninn(endu)

Anupallavi:
andamaina kunda radana
indirA hRn-mandira ninn(endu)

Charanam:
nIdu palukE palukurA
nIdu kulukE kulukurA
nIdu taLukE taLukurA
nijamaina tyAgarAja nuta ninnu-(endu)
