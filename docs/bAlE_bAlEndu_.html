bAlE bAlEndu

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: reeti gaula
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
bAlE bAlEndu bhUShaNi bhava rOga shamani

Anupallavi:
3 phAla lOcani shrI dharma saMvardhani
sakala lOka janani (bAlE)

Charanam:
1. shIlE nanu rakShimpanu

lOlE kanaka maya su-
cElE kAla vairiki priyamaina
illAlavaiyindu velasinanduku
shrI lalitE nI tanayuDani nanu
kushAlugA piluva valenamma (bAlE)

2. sArE sakala nigama vana

parivArE pAmara jana
dUrE kIravANi shrI panca nada pura
vihArivai velasinandukika nA
nEra kOTulanella sahinci
gAravimpa valenamma shivE (bAlE)

3. rAmE praNatArti harAbhi-
rAmE dEva kAminI

nEmamutO pUja sEyani shrI

shyAmaLE dharma saMvardhani (bAlE)
