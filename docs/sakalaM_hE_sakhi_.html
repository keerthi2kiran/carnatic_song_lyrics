sakalaM hE sakhi

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: sindhu bhairavi
Tala: Adi
Language: sanskrit

Pallavi:
sakalaM hE sakhi jAnAmi tata |
prakaTa vilAsaM paramaM dhadhasE ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
alika mRgamada maya maShIkala nO- |

lalitaM tava pallavi tamanasi ni- |
shcalatara mEgha shyAmaM dhadhasE ||

cAru kapOlasthala karAMcita vi- |
cAraM hE sakhi jAnAmi |
nArAyaNa mahinAyaka shayanaM |
shrI ramaNaM tava cittE dhadhasE ||

vinutabhOgasukha vibhavam dhadhasE |
