ErIti nevvaru

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: mukhari
Tala: ?
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
ErIti nevvaru ninnu neTTu bhAviMcinAnu |
vAri vAri pAliki varaduDa vauduvu ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
cEri kolcinavAriki jEpaTTu guMcamavu |
kOri nutiMcuvAri koMgupaiDivi |
mEratO dalacuvAri mETinidhAnamavu |
sArapu vivEkulaku saccidAnaMduDavu ||

kAvalenannavAriki kAmadhEnuvu mari |
sEva cEsEvAriki ciMtAmaNivi |
nIvE gatannavAriki nikhila rakShakuDavu |
vAviri SharaNu vEDE vAriki bhAgyarAshivi ||

ninnu bUjiMcEvAri nija paratattvamavu |
yinniTA nIdAsulaku nElikavu |
yennaga shrIvEMkaTEsha yihaparamulakunu |
panni kAcukunnavAri phaladAyakuDavu ||
