kūsina kaṇḍīryā

English Lyrics

Composer: Purandara Dasa
Raga:
Tala:
Language: kannada

Pallavi:
kūsina kaṇḍīryā guru
mukhyaprāṇana kaṇḍīryā

Anupallavi:

Charanam:

bālana kaṇḍīryā
balavantana kaṇḍīryā ||a.pa ||

añjaneyudaradi puṭṭitu kūsu
rāmana caraṇake eragitu kūsu
sītege uṅgura koṭṭitu kūsu
laṅkāpuravane suṭṭitu kūsu ||

baṇḍi annavanuṇḍitu kūsu
bakana prāṇava konditu kūsu
viṣada laḍḍuge medditu kūsu
maḍadige puṣpava koṭṭitu kūsu ||

māyāvādigaḷa gedditu kūsu
dvaitamatavanuddharisitu kūsu
madhvarāyaremba pesarina kūsu
purandaraviṭhalana prēmada kūsu ||
