graha balamEmi

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: revagupti
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
1 graha balamEmi shrI rAm-
Anugraha balamE balamu

Anupallavi:
graha balamEmi tejO maya
vigrahamunu dhyAnincu vAriki nava(graha)

Charanam:
graha pIDala panca pApamulan-
Agrahamulu gala kAmAdi ripula
nigrahamu jEyu harini bhajincu
tyAgarAjuniki rasikAgrEsarulaku (graha)

Kannada Lyrics
