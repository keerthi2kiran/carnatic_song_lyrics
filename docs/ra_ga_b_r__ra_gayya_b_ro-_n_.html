Composer: Kanaka Dasa

Language: kannada

Pallavi:

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
raṅga bārō raṅgayya bāro- nī
bāradiddare enna prāṇa uḷiyadu
attige maiduna bāro atteya magaḷa gaṇḍa
attige mēlattige nādini soseya gaṇḍa ||1||
māvana aḷiyane bāro māvana bīgana tanuja
māvana maḍadiya magaḷa taṅgiya gaṇḍa ||2||
ambudhi śayanane bāro ādivastuve raṅga
kambadoḷu nelasida ādikēśarāya ||3||
