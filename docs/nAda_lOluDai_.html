nAda lOluDai

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: kalyana vasantam
Tala: rupakam
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
nAda lOluDai brahmA-
nandamandavE manasA

Anupallavi:
swAdu phala prada sapta
swara rAga nicaya sahita (nAda)

Charanam:
hari-harAtma bhU surapati
shara-janma gaNEshAdi
vara maunulupAsinca rE
dhara tyAgarAju teliyu (nAda)

Kannada Lyrics

ಸ್ವಾದು ಫಲ ಪ್ರದ ಸಪ್ತ
