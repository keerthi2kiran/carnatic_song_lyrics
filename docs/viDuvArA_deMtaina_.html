viDuvArA deMtaina

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: ahiri
Tala: ?
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
viDuvArA deMtaina verrivADanaina nIku |
kaDavAru navva kuMDAgAcukO nannunu ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
j~jAnamunE neruga naj~jAnamu nEneruganu |
mAnanu viShhayamulu marigiMtainA |
nI nAmamu noDigi nI dAsuDa nanukoMdu |
dIninE vahiMcu koni tiddukO nannunu ||

akarmamu neruganu sukarmamu neruganu |
prakaTa saMsAramupai pATu mAnanu |
vokapani vADavai pUni mudra dhArinaiti |
mokamODi yiMdukE gOmuna nElu nannunu ||

venukagAnanu muMdu vicAriMci kAnanu |
ninupai dEha dhArinai nIku mokkEnu |
GanuDa shrIvEMkaTEsha kannuleduTa baDiti |
kanipO viDuvarAdu karuNiMci nannunu ||
