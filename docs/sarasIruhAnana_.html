sarasIruhAnana

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: mukhari
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
sarasIruhAnana rAma
samayamu brOva cid-ghana

Anupallavi:

baragErayya tyAgarAja nuta (sara)
