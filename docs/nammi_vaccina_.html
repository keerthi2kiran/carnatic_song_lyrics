nammi vaccina

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: kalyani
Tala: rupakam
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
nammi vaccina nannu nayamuga brOvavE ninnu

Anupallavi:
kommani varamulanosagE kOvUri sundarEsha (nammi)

Charanam:
3 vEda purANAgama shAstrAdulu gumi kUDi
pAdamulanu kana jAlaka batimAli vEDa
nAda rUpa shrI saundarya nAyakI patE bhEda
vAda rahita tyAgarAja varada sundarEsha ninu (nammi)

Kannada Lyrics
