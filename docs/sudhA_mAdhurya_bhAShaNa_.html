sudhA mAdhurya bhAShaNa

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: sindhu ramakriya
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
sudhA mAdhurya bhAShaNa
sudhAkarAnana

Anupallavi:
kathAmRtamucE bahu kAlamu
Akali tIriyunnAnu brOvumu (sudhA)

Charanam:
2 durAtmulagu bhU-kirAtakula
cEra rAdanucu sundarAkAra nI
parAyaNula celimi rA kOru tyAga-
rAja nuta O parAtpara su-guNa (sudhA)

Kannada Lyrics
