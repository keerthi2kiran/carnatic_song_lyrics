gururāyara nambirō

English Lyrics

Composer: Purandara Dasa
Raga:
Tala:
Language: kannada

Pallavi:
gururāyara nambirō mārutiyemba
gururāyara nambiro

Anupallavi:


gururāyara nambi biḍade yāvāgalu
duritava kaḷedu sadgatiya paḍevarella

Charanam:


vanadhiya manōvēgadi laṅghisi mahī-
tanujeya śōkava taridu
vanava bēroḍane kittāḍi ārbhaṭisida
danujara baḍidu laṅkeya tanna sakhagitta ||

kaurava baka hiḍimba kīcakaremba
duruḷa santati neggotti
ghōra pātaki duśyāsanana rakutava
hīri mudadi muravairiya bhajisida ||

jīvēśarondemba durvādiya
bhāvaśāstrava muridu
kōvidarige sadbhāṣya tōrida
dēva purandara viṭhala sēvakanāda ||
