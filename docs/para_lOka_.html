para lOka

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: purikalyani
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
para lOka sAdhanamE manasA

Anupallavi:
1 smara lObha mOhAdi pApulanu
smariyincakE shrI rAma bhajana (para)

Charanam:
jananAdi rOga bhayAdulacE
jagamandu kalgu durAsalacE
tanayAdi bAndhavula bhramacE
tagalanIdu tyAgarAja nutuni bhajana (para)

Kannada Lyrics
