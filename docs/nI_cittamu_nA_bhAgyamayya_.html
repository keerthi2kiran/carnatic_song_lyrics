nI cittamu nA bhAgyamayya

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: vijaya vasanta
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
1 nI cittamu nA bhAgyamayya
nir-upAdhika nI vADanayya

Anupallavi:
yOcinci kAryamu lEdanucun-
okapAri sharaNanukoNTinayya (nI)

Charanam:
para daivamula jUcunantanE
bhAvamandu nIvai paragedavayya
dhara lOna nA sari vAralalO
daya jUDavayya tyAgarAja nuta (nI)
