naMda naMdana

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: desh
Tala: ?
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
naMda naMdana vENunAda vinOdamu- |
kuMda kuMda daMtahAsa gOvardhana dharA ||

Charanam:
rAma rAma gOviMda ravicaMdra lOcana |

rdhUma dhAma karaNa catura bhavabhaMjanA ||

kamala kamalavAsa kamalA ramaNa dEvO- |
ttama tamOguNa satata vidUra |
pramadatpramadAnubhava bhAva karaNa |
