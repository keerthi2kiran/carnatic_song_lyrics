calapAdi rOgamI

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: sindhubhairavi
Tala: ?
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
calapAdi rOgamI saMsAramu nEDu |
baluvaina maMdu viShNu bhakti jIvulaku ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
kIDauTa madineriMgiyu mOha meDaladidi |
pADaina vidhikRtamu balavaMtamu |
IDanE idi mAnpa hitavaina vajrAMgi |
jODuvO hari dalaMcuTa jIvulakunu ||

hEyamani telisi tA niccagiMcI yAtma |
pAyadI rati sukhamu balavaMtamu |
mAya nuggulu cEya mAdhavuni daMcanapu |
rAyivO vairAgya racana jIvulaku ||

polayu duritaMpu roMpulu dannuvaDi muMca |
palumAru janmamI balavaMtamu |
nelavukoni sakalaMbu nirmalamugA kaDugu |
jaladhivO vEMkaTEshvaruDu jIvulaku ||
