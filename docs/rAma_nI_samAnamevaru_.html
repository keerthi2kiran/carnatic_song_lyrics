rAma nI samAnamevaru

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: kharaharapriya
Tala: rupakam
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
rAma nI samAnamevaru
raghu vamshOddhAraka

Anupallavi:
bhAmA maruvampu molaka
bhaktiyanu panjarapu ciluka (rAma)

Charanam:
paluku palukulaku tEne-
loluku mATalADu
sOdaralu gala hari tyAgarAja
kula vibhUSha mRdu su-bhASha (rAma)
