ETisukhamu mari

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: varali
Tala: ?
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
ETisukhamu mari yETisukhamu |
okamATa mAtramu naTamaTamaina sukhamu ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
konasAgu duritamule kUDaina sukhamu |
tanuvicAramulalO dAkonna sukhamu |
panilEniyAsalaku baTTayina sukhamu |
venuka mundara jUDa veragaina sukhamu ||

nindalaku lOnaina nIrasapu sukhamu |
bondikini lancambu puNikETi sukhamu |
kindapaDi parulamuMgili gAcusukhamu |
pandivale tanudAne bratikETi sukhamu ||

dhRtimAli yindariki dInuDagu sukhamu |
matimAli bhaMgamulu marapincu sukhamu |
pati vEMkaTEshu kRpa paDasinadi sukhamu |
itarambulanniyunu EpATi sukhamu ||
