shrI sarasvati hitE

English Lyrics

Composer: muthuswami dikshitar
Raga: manji
Tala: Adi
Language: sanskrit

Pallavi:
shrI sarasvati hitE shivE cidAnandE shiva sahitE

Anupallavi:
vAsavAdi mahitE vAsanAdi rahitE

Charanam:
kAma kOTi nilayE
kara dhRta maNi valayE

guru guhOdayE mAmava sadayE
