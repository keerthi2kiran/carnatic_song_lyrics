cani tODi tEvE

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: hari kambhoji
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
cani tODi tEvE O manasA

Anupallavi:
kanikaramutO kani karamiDi cira
kAlamu sukhamanubhavimpa vEgamE (cani)

Charanam:
patitula brOcu paTTAdhikArini
paramArtha mata vasiShThAnusArini
dyuti nirjita shata shambarArini
dhurINa tyAgarAja hRc-cArini (cani)
