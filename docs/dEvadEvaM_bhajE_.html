dEvadEvaM bhajE

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: hindolam
Tala: Khanda Chapu
Language: sanskrit

Pallavi:
dEvadEvaM bhajE divya prabhAvaM |
rAvaNAsuravairi raghu puMgavam ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
rAjavara shEkharaM ravikula sudhAkaraM |
AjAnu bAhuM nIlAbhra kAyaM |
rAjAri kOdaNDa rAjadIkShA guruM |
rAjIva lOcanaM rAmacandram ||

nIlajImUta sannibha sharIraM ghana vi- |
shAla vakShasaM vimala jalaja nAbhaM |
kAlAhi naga haraM dharma saMsthApanaM |
bhU lalanAdhipaM bhOgashayanam ||

paMkajAsana vinuta parama nArAyaNaM |
shaMkarArjita janaka cApa daLanaM |
laMkA vishOShaNaM lAlita vibhIShaNaM |
vEMkaTEshaM sAdhu vibudha vinutam ||
