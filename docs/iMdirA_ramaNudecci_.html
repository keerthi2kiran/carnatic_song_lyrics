iMdirA ramaNudecci

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: bouli
Tala: ?
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
iMdirA ramaNudecci yiyyarO mAkiTuvale |
poMdi yItanibUjiMpa boddAyanippuDu ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
dhAruNi mairAvaNu daMDiMci rAmudecci |
nErupuna miMcina yaMjanI tanayA |
GoranAga pAShamula goTTivEsI yItani |
kArunyamaMdi naTTi khagarAju garuDA ||

nAnAdEvatalaku narasiMha gaMbhamulO |
pAnipaTTi cUpinaTTi prahlAduDA |
mAnavuDai kRShNa mahimala vishvarUpu |
pUni baMDi nuMcukonna pOTu baMDa yarjunA ||

shrI vallabhunaku nashESha kaiMkaryamula |
shrI vEMkaTAdrivaina shEShamUritI |
kaivasamaina yaTTi kArta vIryArjunuDA yI- |
dEvuni nIvEla niTTitecci mAku niyyarE ||
