vidulaku mrokkeda

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: mayamalava gaula
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
vidulaku mrokkeda saMgIta kO(vidulaku)

Anupallavi:
mudamuna shaMkara kRta sAma nigama
vidulaku nAdAtmaka sapta svara (vidulaku)

Charanam:
kamalA gaurI vAg-IshvarI vidhi
garuDa dhvaja shiva nAradulu
amarEsha bharata kAshyapa caNDIsha
AnjanEya guha gaja mukhulu
su-mRkaNDuja kumbhaja tumburu vara
sOmEshvara shArMga dEva nandI
pramukhulaku tyAgarAja vandyulaku
brahmAnanda sudhAmbudhi marma (vidulaku)
