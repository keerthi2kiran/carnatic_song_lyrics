bhUShAvatIM

English Lyrics

Composer: muthuswami dikshitar
Raga: bhushavathi
Tala: rupakam
Language: sanskrit

Pallavi:
bhUShAvatIM manju bhAShAvatIM bhajE(a)ham

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
tOSha-kara shEkharIM durgAM bRhannAyakIM
(madhyama kAla sAhityam)
shEShAdrIsha sOdarIM shiva guru guha priya-karIm
