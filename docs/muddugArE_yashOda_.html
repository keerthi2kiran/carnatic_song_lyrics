muddugArE yashOda

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: kurinji
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
muddugArE yashOda muMgiTa mutyamu vIDu |
tiddarAni mahimala dEvakI sutuDu ||
