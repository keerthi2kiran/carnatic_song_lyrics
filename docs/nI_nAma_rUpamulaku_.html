nI nAma rUpamulaku

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: saurashtram
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
nI nAma rUpamulaku
nitya jaya maMgaLam

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
1. pavamAna sutuDu paTTu
pAdAravindamulaku (nI)

2. paMkajAkShi nelakonna-
yaMga yugamunaku (nI)

3. nava muktA hAramulu
naTiyincEyuramunaku (nI)

4. naLinAri kEru ciru
navvu gala mOmunaku (nI)

5. prahlAda nAradAdi bhaktulu
pogaDucuNDE (nI)

6. rAjIva nayana tyAgarAja
vinutamaina (nI)
