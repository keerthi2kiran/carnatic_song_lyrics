uNDEdi rAmuDokaDu

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: hari kambhoji
Tala: rupakam
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
1 uNDEdi rAmuDokaDu
Uraka ceDi pOku manasA

Anupallavi:
caNDa mArtANDa madhya
maNDalamunanu celaMgucun(uNDEdi)

Charanam:
tAmasAdi guNa rahituDu
dharmAtmuDu sarva samuDu
kShEma karuDu tyAgarAja
citta hituDu jagamu niNDiy(uNDEdi)
