dAsharathI nI RNamu

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: thodi
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
dAsharathI nI RNamu dIrpa nA
taramA parama pAvana nAma

Anupallavi:
Asha tIra dUra dEshamulanu
prakAshimpa jEsina rasika shirOmaNi (dAsharathI)

Charanam:
bhakti lEni kavi jAla varENyulu
bhAvameruga lErani kalilOna jani
bhukti mukti kalgunani kIrtanamula
bOdhincina tyAgarAja karArcita (dAsharathI)

Kannada Lyrics

ರಾಗ: ತೋಡಿ
ತಾಳ: ಆದಿ
