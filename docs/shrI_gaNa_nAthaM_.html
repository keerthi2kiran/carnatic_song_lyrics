shrI gaNa nAthaM

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: kanakangi
Tala: Adi
Language: sanskrit

Pallavi:
shrI gaNa nAthaM bhajAmyahaM
shrIkaraM cintitArtha phaladam

Anupallavi:
shrI guru guhAgrajam agra pUjyaM
shrI kaNThAtmajaM shrita sAmrAjyaM (shrI)

Charanam:
ranjita nATaka raMga tOShaNaM
shinjita vara maNi-maya bhUShaNaM
AnjanEyAvatAraM subhAShaNaM
kunjara mukhaM tyAgarAja pOShaNam (shrI)
