jJAnulAla yOgulAla

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: mukhari
Tala: ?
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
jJAnulAla yOgulAla sakalaviraktulAlA |
nAnAvidhAla nammEru suNDi ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
alari yintula adharAmRtamu liviyel a |
kAlakUTa viShamu karaNi suNDi |
shIlamutO vIrala ceTTalu paTTuTa lellA |
bAlanAgAla doDiki paTTuTa suNDi ||

kAntalu navvucunainA gannula cUcina cUpu |
pantamuna nalugula pAtara suNDi |
bantula neduTanunna paDatula cannulivi |
kantala noDDina baNDigaNDlu summi ||

javvanapugAminula sarasapu mATalel A |
mavvamaina yaTTi cokku mantrAlu suNDi |
ivvalanu shrIvEMkaTEshwaru dAsula kivi |
civvana jeppinaTTu cEsEvi suNDi ||
