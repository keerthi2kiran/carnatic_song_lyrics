nijamuga nI

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: sahana
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
nijamuga nI mahima telpa lEru

Anupallavi:
bhajana sEyumani palikiri kAni
aja gaja simha shRgAlAntaram(a)ni (nija)

Charanam:
3 buddhiyanu tallini viShaya viTulaku
proddunosagi paikamulArjincE
siddhulu sujanula sabhaku rA nErani
peddalu shrI tyAgarAja vinuta rAma (nija)
