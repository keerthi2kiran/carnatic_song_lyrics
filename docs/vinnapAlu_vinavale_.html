vinnapAlu vinavale

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: bhupalam
Tala: ?
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
vinnapAlu vinavale viMta viMtalu |
pannagapu dOma tera paietta vElayyA ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:

garuDa kinnara yakSha kAminulu gamulai |
