cinna nADE

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: kalaa nidhi
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
cinna nADE nA ceyi paTTitivE

Anupallavi:
enna rAniyUDigamu kaikoni
entO nannu pAlanamu sEtunani (cinna)

Charanam:
iTTi vELa viDanADudAmanO
ElukondAmaniyencinAvO teliya
guTTu brOvavE suguNa vAri nidhi
goppa daivamA tyAgarAja nuta (cinna)
