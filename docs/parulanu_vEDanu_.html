parulanu vEDanu

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: balahamsa
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
parulanu vEDanu patita pAvanuDA

Anupallavi:
kari varadA nIvE kAMkShatO brOvumika (paru)

Charanam:
amara vara nIdu nAmAtishayambE
jIvanamu sEyucunnAnu shrI tyAgarAja vinuta (paru)

Kannada Lyrics
