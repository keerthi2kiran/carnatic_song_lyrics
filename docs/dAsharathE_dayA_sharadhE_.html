dAsharathE dayA sharadhE

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: kokila priya
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
dAsharathE dayA sharadhE

Anupallavi:
Ashara haraNa parAshara gEya
kavIsha su-hRdaya nivEsha ghRNA kara (dA)

Charanam:
sarasija nAbha jala dhara shObha
paripAlitEbha vara bhakta lAbha
paricara kIsha pApa vinAsha
taraNi saMkAsha tyAgarAjEsha (dA)
