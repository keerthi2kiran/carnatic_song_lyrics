ilalO praNatArti

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: athana
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
ilalO praNatArti haruDanucu
pErevariDirE shaMkaruDani nIk(ilalO)

Anupallavi:
talaci karagi cira kAlamu padamuna
daNDamiDina nAyeDa daya lEdAye ( ilalO)

Charanam:
2 kara caraNayuramu nosalu bhujamulu
dharaNi sOka mrokkaga lEdA
sharaNanucunu moraliDa lEdA
panca nadIsha tyAgarAja nuta nIk(ilalO)

Kannada Lyrics

ಪೇರೆವರಿಡಿರೇ ಶಂಕರುಡನಿ ನೀಕ್(ಇಲಲೋ)

ಅನುಪಲ್ಲವಿ:
