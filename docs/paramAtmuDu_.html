paramAtmuDu

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: vagadishwari
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
1 paramAtmuDu veligE muccaTa bAga telusukOrE

Anupallavi:
hariyaTa haruDaTa surulaTa narulaTa
akhilANDa kOTulaTayandarilO (parama)

Charanam:
gaganAnila tEjO-jala bhU-mayamagu
mRga khaga naga taru kOTulalO
saguNamulO viguNamulO satatamu
sAdhu tyAgarAjAdiyAshritulalO (parama)
