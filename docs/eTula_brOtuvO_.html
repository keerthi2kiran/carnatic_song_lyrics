eTula brOtuvO

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: chakravakam
Tala: Triputa
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
eTula brOtuvO teliyadEkAnta rAmayya

Anupallavi:
kaTa kaTa nA caritamu
karNa kaThOramayya (eTula)

Charanam:
vaTTi goDDu rIti bhakShinci tirigiti
puTTu lObhulanu poTTakai pogaDiti
duShTulatO kUDi duSh-kRtyamula salpi
raTTu jEsina tyAgarAjuni dayatO (eTula)
