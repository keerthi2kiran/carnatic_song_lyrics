nIdu caraNamulE

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: simhendra madhyamam
Tala: miSra chApu
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
nIdu caraNamulE gatiyani
nerata nammina vADanurA rAma

Anupallavi:
vEda vEdAnta vidituDani
velayu shrI sarvEsha rAma (nIdu)

Charanam:
pApa karmamu niNDurAyindu
bAdha paDa nE jAlarA
E vagagAnainanu nann-
ElukO tyAgarAja nuta (nIdu)

Kannada Lyrics
