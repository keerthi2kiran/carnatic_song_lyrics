English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: senchuruti
Tala: Adi
Language: sanskrit

Pallavi:
navanItacOra namOnamO |
navamahimArNava namOnamO ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
harinArAyaNa kEshavAcyutakRShNa |

naranArAyaNa namOnamO ||

nigamagOcara viShNu nIrajAkSha vAsudEva |
nagadhara nandagOpa namOnamO |
triguNAtItadEva trivikrama dwAraka |
nagarAdhinAyaka namOnamO ||
