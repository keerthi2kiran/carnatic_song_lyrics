niravadhi sukhada

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: ravichandrika
Tala: Adi
Language: sanskrit

Pallavi:
niravadhi sukhada nirmala rUpa
nirjita muni shApa

Anupallavi:
sharadhi bandhana nata saMkrandana
shaMkarAdi gIyamAna sAdhu mAnasa susadana

Charanam:
mAmava marakata maNi nibha dEha
shrI maNi lOla shrita jana pAla
bhIma parAkrama bhIma karArcita
tAmasa rAjasa mAnava dUra
tyAgarAja vinuta caraNa
