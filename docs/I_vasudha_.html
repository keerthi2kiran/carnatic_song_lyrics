I vasudha

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: sahana
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
I vasudha nIvaNTi daivamunendu kAnarA

Anupallavi:
bhAvukamu kalgi vardhillu
kOvUri sundarEsha girIsha (I)

Charanam:
AsacEyara nimiShamu nI pura
vAsamonara jEyu vAri madi
vesaTalellanu tolaginci
dhana rAsulanAyuvulu
bhUsura bhaktiyu tEjamun(o)sagi
bhuvanamandu kIrti kalga jEsE
dAsa varada tyAgarAja hRdaya
nivAsa cid-vilAsa sundarEsha (I)
