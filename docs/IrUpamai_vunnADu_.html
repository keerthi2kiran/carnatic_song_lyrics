IrUpamai vunnADu

English Lyrics

Composer: annamAcArya
Raga: padi
Tala: ?
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
IrUpamai vunnADu yItaDE parabrahmamu |
shrIramAdEvitODa shrIvEMkaTEshuDu ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
podali mAyAdEvipaTTina samudramu |
