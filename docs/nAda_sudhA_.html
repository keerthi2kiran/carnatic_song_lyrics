nAda sudhA

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: arabhi
Tala: rupakam
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
nAda sudhA rasambilanu
narAkRti yAye manasA

Anupallavi:
vEda purANAgama
shAstrAdulakAdhAramau (nAda)

Charanam:
swaramulArunokaTi ghaNTalu
vara rAgamu kOdaNDamu
dura naya dEshyamu triguNamu
nirata gati sharamurAsarasa saMgati
sandarbhamu-gala giramulurA
dhara bhajana bhAgyamurA
tyAgarAju sEvincu (nAda)
