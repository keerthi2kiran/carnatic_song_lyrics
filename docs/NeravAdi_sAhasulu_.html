Pallavi:

durita vidUrulu dhRvAdulu ||

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
takkaka shrIharibhaktitapAlusEsi yekkiri |
cakkagA vaikuMThamu sanakAdulu |

pekku saMsArajaladhi bhIShmAdulu ||
