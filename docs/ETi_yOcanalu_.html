ETi yOcanalu

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: kiranavali
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
ETi yOcanalu jEsEvurA
eduru palkuvArevaru lErurA

Anupallavi:
nOTi mATa jArcaga rAdurA
kOTi vElpulalO mETiyaina nIv(ETi)

Charanam:
meNDu shUrulalO venuka tIyavani
reNDu mATalADE vADu kAdani
aNDa kOTla pAlincu vADani
caNDa maunulADa tyAgarAja nuta (ETi)

Kannada Lyrics

ನೋಟಿ ಮಾಟ ಜಾರ್ಚಗ ರಾದುರಾ
