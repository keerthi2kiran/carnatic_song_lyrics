shrI kamalAmbikE shivE

English Lyrics

Composer: muthuswami dikshitar
Raga: sri
Tala: khanda ekam
Language: sanskrit

Pallavi:
shrI kamalAmbikE shivE pAhi mAM lalitE
shrI-pati vinutE sitAsitE shiva sahitE

Anupallavi:

Charanam:
rAkA candra mukhI rakShita kOla mukhI
ramA vANI sakhI rAja yOga sukhI
shAkambhari shAtOdari candra kalA dhari
shaMkari shaMkara guru guha bhakta vashaMkari
EkAkShari bhuvanEshwari Isha priya-kari
shrI-kari sukha-kari shrI mahA tripura sundari

ತಾಳ: ಖಂಡ ಏಕ
