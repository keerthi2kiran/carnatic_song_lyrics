lAliyUgavE

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: neelambari
Tala: rupakam
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
lAliyUgavE mApAli daivamA

Anupallavi:
lAliyUgavE nunu gAli tiNDi pAnpu paini (lAli)

Charanam:
pammina vEDuka mIra tammulatOnu
kammani viDemosaguyA janakAtmajatOnu
bomma dEvara taNDri bhUmIshula tODanu
sammatamuna tyAgarAju sannuti sEyaga (lAli)
