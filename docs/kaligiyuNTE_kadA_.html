kaligiyuNTE kadA

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: keeravani
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
kaligiyuNTE kadA kalgunu kAmita phala dAyaka

Anupallavi:
1 kaliniyiMgitamerugaka ninnADukoNTi
calamu cEyaka nA talanu cakkani vrAta (kaligi)

Charanam:
bhAgavatAgrEsarulagu nArada
prahlAda parashara rAmadAsulu
bAguga shrI raghu rAmuni padamula
bhakti jEsina rIti tyAgarAjunikipuDu (kaligi)

Kannada Lyrics
