nA jIvAdhAra

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: bilahari
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
nA jIvAdhAra nA nOmu phalamA

Anupallavi:
rAjIva lOcana rAja-rAja shirO-maNi (nA)

Charanam:
nA cUpu prakAshamA nA nAsika parimaLamA
nA japa varNa rUpamA nAdu pUjA sumamA
tyAgarAja nuta (nA)
