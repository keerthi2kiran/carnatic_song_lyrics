kOri sEvimpa

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: kharaharapriya
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
kOri sEvimpa rArE kOrkalIDEra

Anupallavi:
shrI ramaNI-karamau
kOvUri sundarEshvaruni (kOri)

Charanam:
surulu vEyi vanne baMgAru
virulacE pUjimpaga bhU-
surulu sanakAdi mauni
varulu nutimpaga
sirulittunani koluvaiyuNDE
shrI saundarya nAyikA
varuni shrI tyAgarAja
varaduni paramAtmuni haruni (kOri)
