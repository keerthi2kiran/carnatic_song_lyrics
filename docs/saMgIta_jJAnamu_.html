saMgIta jJAnamu

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: dhanyasi
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
saMgIta jJAnamu bhakti vinA
sanmArgamu kaladE manasA

Anupallavi:
1 bhRMgi naTEsha samIraja ghaTaja
mataMga nAradAdulupAsincE (saMgIta)

Charanam:
nyAyAnyAyamu telusunu jagamulu
mAyAmayamani telusunu durguNa
kAyajAdi ShaD-ripula jayincu
kAryamu telusunu tyAgarAjuniki (saMgIta)
