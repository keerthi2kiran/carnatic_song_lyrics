viDa jAladurA

English Lyrics

Composer: tyAgarAja
Raga: jana ranjani
Tala: Adi
Language: telugu

Pallavi:
viDa jAladurA nA manasu vinarA

Anupallavi:
aDiyAsalacE tagili nEn-
ArtibaDina padambulanu (viDa)

Charanam:
tanuvE panuluku canina mari
kana rAnidi kanugonina
ninugA bhAvinci santasilliti
shrI tyAgarAja nuta (viDa)
